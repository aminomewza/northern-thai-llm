Layer A · lanna_khuang/ ข่วง Khuang · Royal Courtyard "The foundational gathering space where community begins all important activity" $ lanna-khuang · formerly lannaforge · Data Foundation Toolkit
ข่วง
Khuang
"The ข่วงหลวง — the open royal courtyard before a temple or palace"
Raw materials are laid out in the courtyard, organised, and prepared before any craft begins. Layer A takes the raw master Google Sheet and builds the JSONL corpus — everything downstream begins here. The courtyard is where the Lanna-3L benchmark is born.
$ lanna-khuang
salung_builder.py

A traditional Lanna water bowl, often silver or lacquerware, used in rituals for welcoming guests. As the สลุง holds and presents pure water, this module holds raw sheet data and presents a clean, structured JSONL corpus to all downstream layers.

huuk_validator.py

The traditional Lanna floor loom for weaving textiles. หูก weaves consistency checks through the JSONL — every row must follow the correct thread. Acts as the gate: no downstream layer proceeds on violations.

tok_splitter.py

Bamboo strips split for weaving and basketry. ตอก partitions the dataset into train/dev/test splits — cutting the data into clean, even pieces, stratified by data_type and context_dependence.

rak_annotator.py

Lacquer from the rak tree — applied in layers to coat and preserve Lanna lacquerware. รัก applies the annotation codebook to each row, coating raw data with pragmatic meaning: intent, tone, construction type, code-switch.

tung_definer.py

A Lanna Buddhist ritual banner, planted to mark direction and aspiration. ตุง plants flags that mark the 7 semantic directions of the benchmark — the LannaFacets schema. Single source of truth for all field definitions.

Layer B · lanna_kruafai/ ครัวไฟ Krua Fai · Traditional Kitchen "The heart of transformation — where fire shapes everything" $ lanna-kruafai · formerly lannasmith · Adaptation Toolkit
ครัวไฟ
Krua Fai
"The traditional Lanna kitchen — spiritual and architectural heart of the home"
Not just a cooking room but a centre of transformation. Raw ingredients become meals; heat shapes everything. Layer B transforms raw models through zero-shot prompting, few-shot conditioning, and LoRA fine-tuning. The GPU is the fire.
$ lanna-kruafai
khantok_registry.py

A traditional Lanna low-footed serving bowl holding multiple dishes on one tray — gathering and communal sharing. ขันโตก holds the 9-model registry: GPT-4o, Gemini, Claude, DeepSeek, Typhoon-S, Typhoon2, SeaLLM, Llama 3.1 8B, Qwen2.5.

chalu_templater.py

A cut-out stencil pattern used in Lanna wood and metal decoration. ฉลุ constructs prompts from templates — the only place in the codebase where prompts are built. English system prompt + Thai/NTD user turn.

mui_inference/api.py · vllm.py

A traditional hammer used in Lanna silver and metalcraft. มุย strikes the model to produce outputs. Covers frontier APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek) and open-weights via vLLM. Retries on 429/5xx with exponential backoff.

sin_finetuner.py

The ซิ่น is the traditional Lanna tubular skirt — woven with intricate patterns, a symbol of Lanna identity. ซิ่น weaves new patterns into the base model (Typhoon2) without re-weaving the entire garment. PEFT + TRL, r=8, α=16. Paper 1 only.

Layer C · lanna_jorfa/ จ่อฟ้า Jor Fa · Sky Finial "Reaching skyward — connecting the earthly generation to the heaven of perfect accuracy" $ lanna-jorfa · formerly lannascope · Diagnostic Toolkit
จ่อฟ้า
Jor Fa
"The elegant finial atop a Lanna viharn, often carved as a naga"
It reaches skyward, connecting earth to the highest realms — a measuring rod of sacred aspiration. Layer C runs the full LannaDiagnos stack: Triple-ChrF, Dialect Gap Score, LannaFacets slicing, human evaluation, and error analysis.
$ lanna-jorfa
① Automatic Diagnostics
sathu_robustness.py

A Pali-derived word used as "amen" in Lanna Buddhist chanting — an affirmation spoken three times. สาธุ runs the 3P Prompt Robustness protocol, affirming three prompt variations as valid tests. Three affirmations, three prompts.

traiphum_multiref.py

The Buddhist cosmology of three realms (heaven, earth, underworld). ไตรภูมิ scores each hypothesis against 3 references — 3 hypotheses × 3 references = 9 ChrF values via sacrebleu.sentence_chrf.

kaew_aggregator.py

Crystal — clear and refractive. แก้ว aggregates 9 ChrF values into the Triple-ChrF: chrf_max (optimistic ceiling), chrf_avg (expected performance), chrf_diff (prompt sensitivity). Like a prism refracting light into a spectrum.

pong_gap.py

In Lanna, ป่อง refers to a gap or interval — the space between woven bamboo slices. ป่อง computes the headline metric: G = chrf_avg(STD) − chrf_avg(NTD). The gap that defines the benchmark.

tae_slicer.py

A flat woven bamboo panel — used as fencing, walls, or a drying rack for tobacco. The gaps allow airflow between compartments. แตะ slices scores by the 7 LannaFacets metadata axes, each gap a distinct category cell.

② Human Diagnostics
bailan_form.py

The traditional medium for recording Lanna scripture and legal texts — a blank surface awaiting inscription. ใบลาน generates human evaluation forms for annotators: 100 items × 3 annotators, covering Generation, Fluency, and 4 Likert axes.

hompoi_agreement.py

ฮอม = to gather together · ปอย = a merit-making festival. Villagers pool resources for a communal goal — "who helps us, we go help them." ฮอมปอย measures how well annotators pool their judgments into a shared reliability score.

③ Error Analysis
hae_error.py

A traditional Lanna cast net — thrown wide, pulled in to separate catch from water. แห casts a wide net over all outputs and surfaces only the failures: worst-k by chrf_avg, tone-flip, construction-loss, CD-drop, prompt-sensitivity outliers.

Meta · ab_meta/ + Pipeline Runner แอ๊บ · ฟ้อน Ab · Fon · Container & Dance "The people and laws that hold the community together" Shared utilities · formerly lannakit
pujan_io.py

The Lanna lay minister — a former monk who bridges the temple and villagers, carrying messages between realms. ปู่จ๋าน handles all JSONL read/write and SHA-256 hashing: the bridge that carries data in and out.

poluang_orchestrate.py

The Northern Thai village headman — a fatherly figure who leads community activities and liaisons between government and people. ป้อหลวง reads the pipeline YAML and coordinates all layers in order. Used by $ lanna-fon run.

mahin_seed.py

มะหิน — Lanna word for stone. Solid, unchanging, the same every time. seed_all(42) seeds numpy, torch, random, and transformers in one call. The stone anchor that makes every experiment reproducible.

pee_manifest.py

A mark or tag left to identify ownership or provenance. Every experiment produces a ปี้: a manifest stamped with config, data hashes, git SHA, tooling versions, and hardware. No output is accepted without its tag.

heet_schema.py

ฮีต (from Pali charita) — the customary moral codes passed down through Lanna generations, the "right way" of living. ฮีต defines the Pydantic schemas that enforce the right structure for every data row. No row is accepted unless it follows the ฮีต.
